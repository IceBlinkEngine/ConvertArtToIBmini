WALL OF SKULLS Wallset

by Ghaaroth

Wallset is made for Adobe slot. W_skull0.pcx is original work, made from
background of Lords of Empyria website of U.D.L. guild. Other 4 files are
default elements of Adobe wallset, with palette changed to shades of grey to
fit the wallset.

Made using Wallsee and Paint shop pro 32bit shareware.
